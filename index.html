These Lake Houses Are Giving Us FOMO

By Erica Reitman

No matter who you are or where you live, it’s hard not to start dreaming about lazy lake days when the summer months roll in.

In the summer, most of your time will be spent outdoors, so you may as well have lots of space to do it in. Create lots of outdoor hangout space so you can soak up the views.

In any lake house backyard, you can easily create a charming space for you and your family to hang out and enjoy the gorgeous view.
